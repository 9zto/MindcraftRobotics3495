THANK YOU TO OUR SPONSORS

Without our sponsors, our team would be unable to function. Sponsors of our robotics team provide us money and materials. We have different sponsor levels depending on how much our sponsors donate. Each level is provided a different set of benefits for donating. We have four different sponsor levels: Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Bronze. Any sponsor is also entitled to a tax write-off.

Platinum

The Platinum Level is the highest level of sponsor. You become Platinum when you donate anything above $5,000 dollars. A Platinum Sponsor is able to request the robot for any event you are hosting, a large logo on the robot, sponsor banner, and that year's t-shirt, and a miniature 3D printed robot. If you are the highest Platinum Sponsor, you are able to name that year's robot.

Gold

The Gold Level is the second highest level of sponsor. You are Gold when you donate anywhere between $2,501-$5,000 dollars. A Gold Sponsor is able to request the the robot for any event, a large logo on the robot, sponsor robot, and T-shirt, and a miniature 3D printed robot.

Silver

The Silver Level is the third level of sponsor. You are a silver sponsor when you donate anywhere between $1,001-$2,500 dollars. A Silver Sponsor is given a medium logo on the robot, sponsor banner and t-shirt.

Bronze

The Bronze Level is the fourth level of sponsor. You are a Bronze sponsor when you donate up to $1,000 dollars. A Bronze Sponsor is given a small logo on the robot, sponsor banner and t-shirt.

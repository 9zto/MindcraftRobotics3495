Mindcraft Robotics

WELCOME

Team 3495, also known as Mindcraft Robotics, is a high school FRC team located at Edison High School in Fresno, California.

Team Summary

Team 3495 was founded in 2011 at Edison High School. Back then, the team was comprised of only a few students and teachers. Now, Team 3495 has grown to over 30 kids, and multiple teacher and parent mentors. Mindcraft Robotics has won multiple awards and has spread outreach into the community of Fresno, California.

FIRST Power Up

The 2018 game challenge that Team 3495 has prepared a robot for is FIRST Power Up. In the challenge, robots must intake a milk crate and place it on scales and see-saw's present on the field. Additionally, the robots must be able to climb onto a metal foot-long bar eight feet into the air. For this season, Team 3495 will be attending the Las Vegas Regional in Nevada and the Central Valley Regional in Fresno, California.
